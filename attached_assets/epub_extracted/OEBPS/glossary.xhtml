Glossary of Mystical / Contemplative Terms

Contemplation

A quiet form of prayer that focuses on resting in awareness of God's presence rather than speaking many words.

Grace

The unearned love and favor of God given freely to humanity through Christ.

Union with God

The theological concept that believers live in relationship with God through Christ and the indwelling of the Holy Spirit.

Perichoresis

A theological term describing the mutual indwelling and relationship between the Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.

Lectio Divina

An ancient practice of sacred reading where scripture is slowly read, reflected upon, prayed over, and contemplated.

Spiritual Formation

The lifelong process through which a person's character and life are shaped by relationship with God.

Contemplative Prayer

A form of prayer that emphasizes silence, stillness, and attentiveness to God's presence.

Grace-Centered Living

A spiritual posture that focuses on trusting God's love rather than striving to earn approval.
